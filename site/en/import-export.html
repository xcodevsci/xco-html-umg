Import-Export

  • Home
  • Services
  • Import-Export

The receipt or shipment of merchandise by sea requires the intervention of a seasoned professional who takes care of it as soon as it is landed or shortly before boarding a ship.

As an authorized customs agent, we assist you in all administrative and physical operations for both import and export. On the import we offer our services since the launch of your orders until the actual delivery of good through the administrative and especially customs processing of your files. We bring you all our expertise and dynamism in this area to satisfy you sustainably.

In export to allow you to be competitive, we have a team able to provide your external partners with your products in record time. Note that we handle all types of shipments (conventional, custom container, bulk container) and we help you put the goods in customs procedures that suits you.
