Universal Maritime Group is a limited liability company (SARL) operating in the field of maritime consignment, shipping, customs transit, import-export and commercial representation.

Our Activities

Import-Export

The import-export consists in carrying out the coordination and the logistic follow-up which make it possible to realize the operation of purchase and sale concluded between the importer or the exporter and his supplier.

Maritime consignment

The shipping consignee, mandated by the armourer, will carry out the ensemble of administrative operations, technical and commercial operations of the ship at the port.

Transit

The freight forwarder is a service provider that provides a complete service in terms of sea and air freight, assistance in completing customs formalities, project management, warehousing, packaging ...
