Universal Maritime Group is responsible for all your consignment and shipping operations, namely:

Universal Maritime Group, mandated by the shipowner, carries out all the administrative, technical and commercial operations of the ship during its port call. Our mission will be to:

  • Negotiate loading and unloading with material handlers
  • Supervise goods receipt and delivery operations
  • Perform administrative formalities (documents for export, title deeds, declaration to customs)
  • Search, if necessary, new customers to increase the volume of cargo
  • Allocate stopover expenses between freight forwarder, shipper and shipowner
  • Refuel the ship with water and fuel
  • Organize the medical examination of the crew
  • Maintain the boat

Universal Maritime Group has great expertise in maritime consignment:

  • Transportation techniques
  • Maritime law
  • Customs procedures
  • litigation
  • English
  • Dynamism and responsiveness
  • Sense of organization
  • Great rigor
  • Relational capacities
