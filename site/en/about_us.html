Universal Maritime Group is a limited liability company (LLC) engaged in the field of maritime consignment, shipping, customs transit, import-export and commercial representation.

To become the essential partner of the actors of the maritime and port sector in the accompaniment and the taking care of the following activities: consignment, transit fueling, logistics, handling.


We put our expertise, our professionalism and especially the experience of our highly qualified staff at the disposal of our customers, to offer them consignment and transit services adapted to their needs and constraints.

Our values

Encourage and strengthen the professionalism and commitment of customers.
Create an environment conducive to teamwork.
Establish relationships based on mutual trust with customers.
Increase proximity with our customers, understand and satisfy our needs.
Treat the files as quickly as possible and at a lower cost in order to avoid the costs of detentions (shopping and demurrage).
Advise and accompany importers for the control of import-export and transit operations.
